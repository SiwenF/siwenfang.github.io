Understanding the Landscape

I audited every product surface to map UI patterns, quantify fragmentation, and surface where inconsistency was costing the team time.

Tokens, Handover & Shared Language

I paired with front-end to define tokens that map directly to code—removing the “translation layer” that slowed handover.

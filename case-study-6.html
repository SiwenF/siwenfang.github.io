Withdraw Status

Leveraging iOS Live Activities & Dynamic Island to give crypto users real-time withdraw tracking — without ever opening the app.

The Problem

Users leave the app after confirming a withdrawal — then anxiously check back every few minutes.

Crypto withdrawals take time. Network confirmations, security checks, processing queues. The standard pattern — a push notification when it’s done — leaves users in the dark during the wait. What if iOS itself could show the status, live?

About

Designing with empathy and intention.

With over 10 years in design — rooted in landscape architecture and refined through 7 years in tech — I bring a unique perspective to digital product design. I've worked across banking, portfolio management enterprise platforms, and crypto exchanges, tackling complex financial systems head-on.
